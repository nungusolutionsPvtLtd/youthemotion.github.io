About
A friend that is there to make your life better and grow

Youth Emotion is a movement started by young minds who have seen the need to help the groeing generation of youths that have no one to help them navigate through life and preparing for adulthood.A that is willing to save the generations and build it together as a whole. You are not alone in the world and Youth Emotion is there to help you as a friend,brother,sister so that we make the world a better place. Youth Emotion is there to tackle the various Sustainable Development Goals so as to impact and change the future. If you need to talk we are there for you. If you need a friend we are there to help. Be part of a bigger family that is aimed at shaping your future and life.
